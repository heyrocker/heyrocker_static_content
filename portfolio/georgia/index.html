The problem

Georgia had built a digital platform to serve state agencies and provide a consistent experience for constituents, but it was showing its age. Their content architecture did not support modern omnichannel needs, editors wanted modern page building and file management features, and there was no coherent design system.

My role

I led a team of three that designed the new content architecture and editorial tools for Georgia’s new platform, GovHub. I also acted as the representative for content strategy in a cross-functional team of developers, project managers, designers, and client stakeholders.

What we did

  • Content audit, scripts for slicing up content and finding migration issues
  • Interviews with authors
  • Designing the content model
  • Testing new editorial interfaces with authors
  • Designing a common taxonomy system

How it came out

  • All agency sites were migrated into the new system cleanly
  • Original site had 80 agencies, new site grew to 120
  • GovHub became the gold standard for state government technology
